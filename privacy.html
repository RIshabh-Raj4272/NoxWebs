Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026. Your privacy matters to us at NoxWebs.

NoxWebs ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website development services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you contact us or place an order, we collect your name, email address, phone number (+91), and business details.

Project Information: Website content, images, logos, brand guidelines, and specific requirements you provide for development.

Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited on our site (via analytics).

Payment Information: We do not store card details. Payments via UPI, PayPal, or bank transfer are processed through secure third-party providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

• To deliver website development services and communicate about your project

• To process payments and send invoices

• To provide customer support and revisions

• To improve our services and website experience

• To showcase completed work in our portfolio (only with your permission)

• To send project updates (we do not send marketing spam)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We may share data only with:

• Payment processors (PayPal, banks) to complete transactions

• Hosting providers if we deploy your website (only with your consent)

• Legal authorities if required by Indian law

All third parties are required to protect your data.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do NOT use cookies.

NoxWebs is 100% cookie-free. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking technologies.

We do not track your browsing behavior, store preferences, or collect analytics data.

Your visit is completely private.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

• Encrypted communication (HTTPS)

• Secure storage of project files

• Limited access to personal information

• Regular backups

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You share information at your own risk.

6. Data Retention

• Project files: Retained for 6 months after delivery for support purposes, then archived

• Contact information: Kept as long as needed for business communication

• Invoices: Retained for 7 years as per Indian tax laws

You can request deletion of your data anytime by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the personal data we hold about you

• Request correction of inaccurate information

• Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

• Opt-out of portfolio showcase

• Withdraw consent for data processing

Contact us at NoxWebs@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to YouTube, Discord, GitHub, or client websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
