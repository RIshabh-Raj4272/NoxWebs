Standard Delivery Times

Delivery starts ONLY after:

✓ 50% advance payment received

✓ All content provided (text, images, logo, brand colors)

✓ Project requirements confirmed


Basic Portfolio/Landing ($10-$50): 5-7 business days

Modern Portfolio/Landing ($50-$100): 7-9 business days

Business Website ($100-$300+): 9-14 business days

Business days = Monday to Saturday (we work Saturdays). Sundays off.

What You Receive

Complete website files:

• All HTML, CSS, JavaScript files

• All images and assets optimized

• Organized folder structure

• README with setup instructions


Delivery methods:

• ZIP file via Google Drive / email

• OR free deployment to your hosting

• OR GitHub repository (on request)

We do NOT provide: Hosting, domain, or ongoing content updates (available separately)

What Causes Delays

Your timeline extends if:

• Late content: You don't provide text/images on time (+1-3 days)

• Late payment: Advance not received (+delay until paid)

• Late feedback: You take 5 days to review preview (+5 days)

• Scope changes: "Can we add a blog?" mid-project (+3-7 days)

• Multiple revision rounds: Sending changes one-by-one (+2-4 days)

• Unclear requirements: Changing mind after build starts (+restart time)

Pro tip: Provide everything upfront and respond within 24 hours to stay on schedule.
