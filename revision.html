What's Included

Revisions ARE for:

• Color changes and adjustments

• Text content updates and corrections

• Image replacements (you provide images)

• Spacing, padding, and alignment tweaks

• Font size and style adjustments

• Button style changes

• Fixing bugs or errors

• Mobile responsiveness fixes

• Minor layout adjustments within same structure

What's NOT Included

Revisions are NOT for:

• Complete redesign or new design direction

• Adding entirely new pages (not in original scope)

• Adding new features or functionality

• Changing from single-page to multi-page (or vice versa)

• Complete content rewrite (we'll place text, you provide it)

• Changing core structure after approval

• Animations not discussed initially

These count as new work and will be quoted separately.

Revision Timeline

When to request: Within 7 days of delivery (14 days for Business package)

Response time: We acknowledge within 12 hours

Completion: Most revisions done within 24-48 hours

After window: Revisions still possible at $1 each, or consider maintenance plan

Important: Revision window starts when we send final files, not when you first view them.

How to Request Revisions

Best way: Send one clear email to NoxWebs@gmail.com with:

1. Subject: "Revision Request - [Your Project Name]"

2. Numbered list of changes (e.g., "1. Change header color to blue, 2. Update phone number")

3. Screenshots with annotations if helpful

4. All changes in ONE email (not 10 separate messages)

Pro tip: Group all feedback together. Sending 5 separate revision requests uses 5 of your revisions. One organized list = 1 revision.

Common Mistakes

• Scope creep: "Can you just add a blog?" = new feature, not revision

• Late feedback: Waiting 3 weeks then requesting changes = extra charge

• Vague requests: "Make it pop more" — please be specific

• Multiple rounds: Sending 1 change, then another, then another wastes your revisions

• New content after approval: Providing all text upfront saves revisions

FAQ

Q: What if I need more than 5 revisions?
A: Extra revisions are $1 each, or we can discuss a small maintenance package.

Q: Do you count small typo fixes as a revision?
A: No. Minor typos and bugs we fix free, even after revision window.

Q: Can I save revisions for later?
A: No, revisions must be used within the window period after delivery.

Q: What if I don't use all my revisions?
A: Unused revisions don't carry over or refund. Use them to perfect your site!

Tips for Success

1. Provide everything upfront — all text, images, brand colors

2. Review on mobile and desktop before requesting revisions

3. Be specific — "darker blue #1e40af" not "different blue"

4. Consolidate feedback — get team input, send once

5. Think about your users — not just personal preference
