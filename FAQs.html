Help & FAQs

What services does NoxWebs offer?

We specialize in modern web development including Portfolio Websites, Business Websites, Landing Pages, Responsive Design, and UI/UX Design. Every project includes clean code, mobile optimization, and user-friendly experiences.

How much does a website cost?

Our pricing is budget-friendly and transparent:

Basic Portfolio / Landing Page: $10 - $50

Modern Portfolio / Landing Page: $50 - $100

Business Website: $100 - $300+

Final price depends on pages, features, and customization level. Contact us for a custom quote.

What's included in each package?

Basic: Responsive Design, Basic Animations, Contact Section, Modern Layout

Modern: Advanced UI Design, Smooth Animations, Multiple Sections, Better Styling

Business: Professional Business Design, Responsive Layout, Multiple Pages, Custom Sections

All packages include mobile optimization and clean code.

How long does it take to deliver my website?

Delivery time depends on complexity:

• Basic Landing Page: 5-7 days

• Modern Portfolio: 7-9 days

• Business Website: 9-14 days

We provide timeline estimates before starting. See our Delivery Policy for details.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes. All packages include free revisions to ensure you're satisfied. Basic includes 2 revisions, Modern includes 3 revisions, and Business includes 5 revisions. Additional revisions can be added. See Revision Policy.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Responsive Development is our specialty. Every website we build works seamlessly across all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. It's included in every package at no extra cost.

What is your payment process?

We work with a 50% advance to start the project and 50% upon completion before delivery. We accept UPI, PayPal, and bank transfer. Full details in our Payment System page.

Do you provide hosting and domain?

We focus on design and development. Hosting and domain are not included in the base price, but we can guide you to purchase them and will deploy your website for free on your hosting.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes. We can modernize your current website with a fresh UI/UX, responsive layout, and performance optimization. Pricing depends on current site structure — contact us with your URL for a quote.

How do I get started?

Simple: Email us at NoxWebs@gmail.com or call +91 1234567890 with your requirements. We'll discuss your vision, suggest the best package, share a timeline, and start after the advance payment.
